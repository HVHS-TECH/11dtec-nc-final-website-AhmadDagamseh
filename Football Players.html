Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi has won a record eight Ballon d'Or awards and led Argentina to victory in the 2022 FIFA World Cup. He is the all-time top scorer for both Barcelona and the Argentina national team, racking up over 800 career goals. Additionally, he holds the record for the most trophies in football history, with over 44 collective titles to his name.

Zinedine Zidane

Zinedine Zidane is one of the few figures to reach the pinnacle of football as both a player and a manager, famously winning the 1998 World Cup and the 2002 Champions League on the pitch before leading Real Madrid to an unprecedented three consecutive Champions League titles from the dugout. His individual brilliance earned him the 1998 Ballon d'Or and three FIFA World Player of the Year awards, cementing his legacy as a tactical mastermind and one of the greatest midfielders in the history of the sport
